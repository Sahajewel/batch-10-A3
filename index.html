Building Amenities

Building Amenities (Unique Section)

01

Parking Space

A parking space is a designated area where vehicles, such as cars, motorcycles, and bicycles, can be temporarily stationed. These spaces are typically found in parking lots, garages, or along streets, and are marked with lines or other indicators to show the boundaries of each space.

02

Swimming Poll

Swimming pools are designed spaces filled with water, used for various activities such as swimming, diving, water sports, and relaxation. They come in various shapes, sizes, and designs, ranging from small, private pools in residential backyards to large, Olympic-sized pools used for competitions.

03

Private security

Private security refers to security services provided by private companies or individuals, rather than by governmental law enforcement agencies. These services are designed to protect people, property, and assets from various threats, including theft, vandalism, and unauthorized access. Private security plays a crucial role in enhancing safety in both residential and commercial settings.

04

Medical Center

A medical center is a healthcare facility that provides a wide range of medical services to patients. These centers often include various specialized departments, offering both outpatient and inpatient care. They serve as a hub for diagnosing, treating, and managing various health conditions, providing essential medical services to the community.

05

Library Area

A library area is a designated space, either within a larger facility or as a standalone building, that is dedicated to the storage, organization, and access of books, digital media, and other informational resources. Libraries serve as essential hubs for learning, research, and community engagement, offering a quiet environment for reading, studying, and intellectual exploration.

06

Kids Playground

A kids' playground is a specially designed outdoor or indoor area where children can engage in play and physical activities. These spaces are essential for child development, providing a safe environment where kids can explore, exercise, and interact with their peers. Playgrounds are equipped with various play structures and equipment that encourage physical activity, creativity, and social interaction.
